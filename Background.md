This work was focused on using QUCAT python library to help electronic engineering students understand quantum circuits and aid them in implementing actual circuits. 
A series of learning notes and lab manual was created for the same. This is a research based on the work mentioned below.

REFERENCE: 

QuCAT is currently developped and maintained by Mario Gely in the group of Gary Steele at the University of Delft in the Netherlands.

Technical paper for qucat  : https://arxiv.org/pdf/1908.10342.pdf.
